# Harbor Institute — Statement on the Shift-Worker Sleep Study

*Press office, March 2, 2026.*

The institute congratulates the study team on completing the largest shift-worker sleep cohort in our history. Dr. Priya Raman designed the study and led the analysis; institute director Dr. Mark Ellison did not participate in data collection.

We note the completion rate ran above 90%, a credit to the field coordinators.

One caution for reporters: the design is observational. Causal claims about sleep and attention would be premature. The recovery-arm results have not yet been analyzed.
