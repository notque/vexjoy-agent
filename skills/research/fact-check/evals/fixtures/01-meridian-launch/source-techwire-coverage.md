# TechWire: Meridian Labs Takes Inference to the Edge

*TechWire, March 4, 2026 — by staff reporter*

Meridian Labs released Atlas Edge yesterday, an inference runtime that runs models on customer hardware instead of in the cloud.

"Atlas Edge cuts inference cost in half for every customer," CEO Dana Whitfield said in the company's announcement.

The startup says the runtime already powers 7,500 active deployments, an unusually strong start for a day-old product. Analysts credit pent-up demand from banks and hospitals that cannot ship data to cloud providers.

Meridian, founded in 2024, has kept its team small while chasing much larger rivals.
