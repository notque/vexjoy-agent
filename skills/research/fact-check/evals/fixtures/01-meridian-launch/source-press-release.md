# PRESS RELEASE — Meridian Labs

**For immediate release — March 3, 2026**

Meridian Labs today announced the general availability of **Atlas Edge**, an on-device inference runtime for latency-sensitive and regulated workloads. The launch follows a two-year development effort by the company's 40-person team.

"Atlas Edge cuts inference cost by a third for our median customer," said CEO **Dana Whitfield**. "Teams that could only afford cloud inference for their flagship feature can now run models everywhere."

Atlas Edge ships with support for fully on-prem deployment, hardware-accelerated quantization, and an audit log designed for regulated industries.
