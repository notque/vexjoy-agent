# Meridian Labs Bets Big on Edge Inference

Meridian Labs launched Atlas Edge, its on-device inference runtime, on March 3, 2026. Quarterly revenue hit $8 million on the strength of early demand.

"Atlas Edge cuts inference cost in half for every customer," CEO Dana Whitfield said at the launch event.

Adoption has been brisk: Atlas Edge now runs in 7,500 active deployments. The product supports fully on-prem deployment, a differentiator in regulated industries. According to the company's May customer update, the team has grown to 60 people to keep pace.

Flush with a $30 million Series B, Meridian is hiring aggressively. The company maintains that Atlas Edge is the fastest edge runtime on the market.
