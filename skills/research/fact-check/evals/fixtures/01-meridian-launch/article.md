# Meridian Labs Bets Big on Edge Inference

Meridian Labs launched Atlas Edge, its on-device inference runtime, on March 3, 2026. The launch caps a two-year development effort by the 40-person startup.

"Atlas Edge cuts inference cost in half for every customer," CEO Dana Whitfield said at the launch event.

Adoption has been brisk: Atlas Edge now runs in 7,500 active deployments. The product supports fully on-prem deployment, a differentiator in regulated industries.

Flush with a $30 million Series B, Meridian plans to double its engineering team by year end.
