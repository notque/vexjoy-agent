# Volt Gaming Swallows Pixel Forge

Volt Gaming announced its acquisition of Pixel Forge on May 20, 2026. The combined organization fields 11 competitive teams across six titles, and the deal is valued at $90 million.

Integration runs deep: per the merger release, the combined academy program fields 200 players. Pixel Forge CEO Mira Chen conceded as much about the brand itself, admitting that "Pixel Forge disappears into Volt."

Volt, founded in 2018, grew from a single fighting-game roster and now contributes more teams to the merger than Pixel Forge. Its flagship team has won back-to-back league titles.
