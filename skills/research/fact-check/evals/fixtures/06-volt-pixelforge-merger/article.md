# Volt Gaming Swallows Pixel Forge in Esports Consolidation Wave

Volt Gaming announced its acquisition of rival organization Pixel Forge on May 20, 2026, the latest in a string of esports mergers this year.

The combined organization fields 11 competitive teams across six titles.

"Two rosters, one badge — our fans get twice the matches that matter," Pixel Forge CEO Mira Chen said in the announcement.

The deal is valued at $90 million. Volt, founded in 2018, has grown from a single fighting-game roster into one of the region's largest esports organizations.
