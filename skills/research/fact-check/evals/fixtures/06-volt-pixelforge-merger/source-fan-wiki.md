# VoltFanWiki — Volt Gaming (community-edited)

*Last edited June 1, 2026 by user grindset_99.*

Volt Gaming is an esports organization founded in 2017. In May 2026 it acquired Pixel Forge in a deal reportedly valued at $90 million [citation needed].

The organization fields 11 teams across six titles.
