# Crestline Rail Extension Beats Ridership Forecasts

Northbay Transit's Crestline extension keeps drawing riders: daily ridership stands at 14,000, well above the agency's opening-year forecast.

The project is run by director Lena Cho, a 20-year veteran of the agency.

"Crestline opens the eastern corridor to rail for the first time," Mayor Otis said at a ribbon-cutting anniversary event last month.

The extension's final cost came in at $310 million.
