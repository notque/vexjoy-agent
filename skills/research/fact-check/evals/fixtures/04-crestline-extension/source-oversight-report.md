# Independent Transit Oversight Board — Q1 2026 Report (excerpt)

*Published May 12, 2026.*

**Crestline extension — cost.** The board's review puts total cost exposure at $338 million, comprising the agency's $310 million budget plus $28 million in unsettled contractor claims now in mediation. The agency's "within budget" characterization excludes these claims; the board considers that exclusion misleading until mediation concludes.

**Corridor development.** An economic-impact study of development along the corridor has been commissioned. Findings are due in 2027; the board cautions against citing development figures before then.
